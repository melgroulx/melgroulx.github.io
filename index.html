I'm Mel! I am a wife and mom of two amazing boys from London, Ontario. I've always had a passion for painting and drawing, and I delved into digital arts over 15 years ago. Poppy Row Designs is a tribute to my family's first home which was on Flanders Row, an area with old war homes. I was inspired by the memorial aspect of poppies and I love to create art that symbolizes a loved-one, a memory, or a monumental day in your life.
